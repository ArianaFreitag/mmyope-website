les éditions MMYOPE

ABOUT

mmyope is a group, started in nov 2020, functioning as a living collaboration between artists in and around nyc.


the result of this collaboration is a series of books led by the intuition that production leads to context, so that the more we make the more this’ll all make sense.


part art-book, part bande-dessiné.

all yours to enjoy.


made up of poems, drawings, prints, collages, ml exercises. put together to tell stories we took to heart.

a publisher of all things mmyope.

mission

there’s no reason for anything to be made in a vacuum. we certainly aren’t interested in making the attempt.


we feel compelled to share with you what drives us, motivates our decisions, and where we look to for insight :


  • books
  • artworks
  • music
  • les éditions de minuit, a continuing reminder of restraint's role in elegance.
  • the kindness of our friends and families. their willingness to participate, engage, and respond to this work is why we're making it in the first place.
